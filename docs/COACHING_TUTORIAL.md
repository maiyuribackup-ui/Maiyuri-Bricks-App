# Coaching Module - Staff Tutorial

A step-by-step guide for using the AI-powered coaching feature in Maiyuri Bricks Lead Management.

---

## What is the Coaching Module?

The Coaching Module uses AI to analyze your lead management performance and provide personalized recommendations to help you improve. It looks at:

- How many leads you handle
- Your conversion rate (leads turned into customers)
- How quickly you respond to new leads
- Your follow-up completion rate
- Hot lead handling

---

## Accessing the Coaching Page

### Step 1: Log In

1. Open your browser and go to: `https://maiyuri-bricks-app-web-llyj.vercel.app`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

### Step 2: Navigate to Coaching

1. After logging in, you'll see the dashboard
2. Look at the left sidebar menu
3. Click on **Coaching** (or navigate to `/coaching`)

---

## Understanding the Team View

When you first open the Coaching page, you'll see the **Team Overview**:

### Team Metrics Card

| Metric                | What It Means                                 |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Leads Handled**     | Total leads managed by the team this period   |
| **Conversion Rate**   | Percentage of leads converted to customers    |
| **Avg Response Time** | Average time to first contact with a new lead |
| **Follow-up Rate**    | Percentage of scheduled follow-ups completed  |

### Top Performers

The page shows the top 3 team members based on their performance score. Each card shows:

- **Name** - Staff member's name
- **Performance Score** - A score from 0-100 based on all metrics
- **Trend** - Whether performance is improving, stable, or declining

---

## Viewing Your Individual Coaching

### Step 1: Select Your Profile

1. Find your name in the **Top Performers** list
2. Click on your card to see detailed coaching

### Step 2: Review Your Performance Score

Your individual view shows:

```
Performance Score: 75/100
```

- **80-100**: Excellent - Keep up the great work!
- **60-79**: Good - Some areas for improvement
- **Below 60**: Needs attention - Focus on recommendations

### Step 3: Read Your AI Recommendations

The AI analyzes your performance and provides **5 personalized recommendations**:

Each recommendation includes:

- **Priority** (High/Medium/Low) - Which to focus on first
- **Area** - What aspect of your work this relates to
- **Action** - Specific steps you can take
- **Expected Impact** - How this will improve your performance

**Example Recommendation:**

```
Priority: High
Area: Response Time
Action: Aim to contact new leads within 2 hours of assignment
Expected Impact: 30% improvement in lead engagement
```

## How to Use the Recommendations

### Daily Actions

1. **Check coaching weekly** - Review your recommendations every Monday
2. **Focus on HIGH priority first** - These have the biggest impact
3. **Track your progress** - Note improvements over time

### Improvement Tips by Area

#### Response Time

- Enable notifications for new lead assignments
- Set aside 30 minutes each morning for new leads
- Use quick response templates

#### Conversion Rate

- Follow up more frequently with interested leads
- Ask qualifying questions early
- Provide accurate pricing quickly

#### Follow-up Completion

- Use the calendar reminders feature
- Schedule follow-ups when creating notes
- Review pending follow-ups each morning

#### Hot Lead Handling

- Prioritize leads marked as "Hot"
- Respond to hot leads within 1 hour
- Keep detailed notes on hot lead interactions

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often is coaching data updated?

**A:** The coaching module analyzes your performance data in real-time. Recommendations are generated fresh each time you view the page.

### Q: Why is my score low even though I'm working hard?

**A:** The score considers multiple factors. Check your recommendations to see which specific areas need improvement. Sometimes it's about quality (conversion) not just quantity (leads handled).

### Q: Can my manager see my coaching data?

**A:** Yes, managers can view team coaching data to help identify areas where the team can improve together.

### Q: What if I disagree with a recommendation?

**A:** The AI provides suggestions based on data patterns. If a recommendation doesn't fit your situation, discuss it with your manager. Your context matters!

### Q: How can I improve my performance score quickly?

**A:** Focus on:

1. Responding to leads quickly (within 2 hours)
2. Completing all scheduled follow-ups
3. Keeping detailed notes on every interaction
4. Prioritizing hot leads

## Troubleshooting

### Page Won't Load

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
3. Clear browser cache and try again
4. Contact IT if issue persists

### No Data Showing

- You need at least 1 week of activity for coaching data
- Check if you're logged into the correct account
- Contact your manager if data should be present

### Recommendations Seem Wrong

- The AI needs sufficient data to provide accurate insights
- Ensure your lead notes and activities are recorded properly
- Wait for more data to accumulate (2+ weeks recommended)
